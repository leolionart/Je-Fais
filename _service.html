Popular Questions

  •   What size are your sample gowns?

We typically have samples in sizes 4 & 12 but it varies by style. During your appointment, your stylist will assist you with your measurements and provide sizing advice. Je Fais wedding gowns are usually comparative to ready-to-wear sizing.

  •    What can I expect from an appointment?

After scheduling an appointment, you'll be assigned a stylist who will guide you through the gown selection process. At your appointment, you'll receive one-on-one attention for one hour in our private salon area.

  •   Do you provide Alteration services?

Je Fais does not offer alterations services. We recommend working with a skilled, seasoned tailor with a background in bridal tailoring.

  •   May we bring a bottle of wine?

To avoid spills and maintain the integrity of the product, we ask that you refrain from bringing outside beverages. However, we are happy to provide refreshments upon request.
