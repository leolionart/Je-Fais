I was planning to sing prior to the reading of our vows to each other, however, I wound up singing my bride down the aisle. A last minute change forced the issue. The original person slated to sing while Christina walked down the aisle was stuck in traffic and unable to make the ceremony. We improvised, although my wife did not know anything was wrong. That was until the doors of the church opened and she started to see me singing to her as her father walked her between the pews. Thinking I’d changed the plans on her, she was a bit confused, and later told me she was thinking I had made this alteration to the program. But she forgave me when I wooed her to my rendition of Jimmy Needham’s song “Light Of Day”.
